Into the Forest

Every path begins in the dark.

You know this hour. The questions get loud in it. A fork in the road, a choice you keep circling, the thing you already know but haven't said out loud yet.

Then a fox slips out from behind the trees. Russet coat, amber eyes, quiet as the moonlight. He doesn't startle and he doesn't rush. He just falls into step beside you and sits, the way something does when it already knows where the trail bends.

This is your guide. He won't let you hide, and he won't let you lie to yourself. What he offers is honesty, a little mischief, and a light for the darker turns of the trail. He has been listening to these woods, and the people who wander them, for over thirty years.

When you're ready, the path is yours. Up and forward, the way you always have, even with a tired and battered heart.

Your guide has a name. Branden Lyesmith.

The Storyteller's Hollow

Meet Branden

Branden has been reading since he was six years old, when a deck of playing cards found its way into his hands and the meanings simply arrived, like a lightbulb over his head. A natural clairvoyant, clairaudient, and clairsentient, he grew up communing with trees and learning to feel the cards without ever turning them over.

He is a blunt, honest, genuine reader. He will not let you hide or lie to yourself, and he'll leave you with comfort, clarity, and usually a laugh. His specialties are yearly readings that map your year month by month, and decision-making readings for when two or more paths lie before you.
